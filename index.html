About Avenor

A transportation partner built for dependable movement.

Avenor Holdings LLC DBA Avenor Transport coordinates import, export, and domestic transportation for shippers and carriers across the United States. We help connect cargo requirements with the right carrier capacity while keeping communication clear from pickup to delivery.

Our team handles shipment coordination, carrier network management, proactive status updates, and on-time delivery planning so customers can move cargo with confidence.
